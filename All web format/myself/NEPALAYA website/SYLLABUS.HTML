Syllabus under TU course

The syllabus for various programs under Tribhuvan University (TU) is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the respective fields. It includes a wide range of subjects tailored to meet the academic and professional needs of students. For instance, the BCA program covers topics like IT fundamentals, programming, web technology, and advanced computing, while the CSIT program focuses on software engineering, operating systems, and artificial intelligence. Similarly, programs like BBA, BBS, and BHM emphasize business management, finance, and hospitality, ensuring students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to excel in their careers.
