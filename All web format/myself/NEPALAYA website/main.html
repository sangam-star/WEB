TRIBHUVAN UNIVERSITY (TU):

Nepalaya is an established private educational institute, and its entire Undergraduate/Bachelor Programs & Master Program are affiliated with Tribhuvan University, Nepal. Tribhuvan University is the only one globally recognized government University of Nepal whilst joining Nepalaya for the further higher studies is one of the greatest achievements in a student’s life. Nepalaya is offering undergraduate programs - BSc CSIT, BBM, BCA, BBS and Master Program – MBS under the affiliation of Tribhuvan University, Nepal.


Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)


The BCA program at Nepalaya College is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer applications and software development. This four-year program equips students with the skills needed to excel in the IT industry, focusing on programming, database management, and web development. Graduates are prepared for careers in software engineering, system analysis, and IT consultancy.


Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc CSIT)


The BSc CSIT program offers an in-depth understanding of computer science and information technology. Students gain expertise in programming, data structures, algorithms, and system design. This program prepares graduates for roles in software development, data analysis, and IT project management, ensuring they are ready to meet the demands of the tech-driven world.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)


The BBA program focuses on developing leadership and managerial skills in students. It covers a wide range of business disciplines, including marketing, finance, human resources, and entrepreneurship. Graduates of this program are well-equipped to take on roles in corporate management, business consultancy, and entrepreneurial ventures.


Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)


The BBS program is tailored to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of business and management principles. It emphasizes practical knowledge in areas such as accounting, economics, and organizational behavior. Graduates are prepared for careers in banking, accounting, and business administration.


Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM)


The BHM program is designed for students aspiring to build a career in the hospitality and tourism industry. It provides hands-on training in hotel operations, food and beverage management, and customer service. Graduates are equipped to work in hotels, resorts, and travel agencies, both locally and internationally.
